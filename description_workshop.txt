English:
[h1]Verbal Attacks: A Harmful Presence.[/h1]
[b]Through this Mod, I hope to make all players aware of the harm caused by verbal attacks.[/b]
[spoiler]Reason for creating this Mod: I was deeply hurt by someone's words, so I made this Mod in a fit of anger.[/spoiler]
Verbal attacks can sometimes be unintentional, yet we must remain vigilant.
Now, you can wield this overpowered Mod to verbally assault all hostile NPCs.
But what will the consequences be? Who knows?
How much invisible damage do these intangible attacks inflict when people hurl insults at each other?
Who truly understands? Perhaps only the victims themselves truly know.
